{# ====================================================== CAPABILITIES (04) #}
CHAPTER — CAPABILITIES 04 BUILDING BLOCKS

Built on solid ingredients. Nothing speculative.

F.01

Whisper-grade transcription

Industry-leading accuracy across accents, domains, and audio quality. Exports as plain text, SRT, VTT, or structured segment JSON.

F.02

Speaker diarization

Opt in with AssemblyAI to separate who said what. Speaker labels flow through the transcript, the recap, and every export.

F.03

Recap drafts that sound like you

Not a summary — a real follow-up. Decisions, next steps, and owners, structured so you can edit and send in under a minute.

F.04

One-click follow-up

Deliver the recap to attendees via SMTP or Resend. Your sending infrastructure, your deliverability — no rebuilt wheels.
