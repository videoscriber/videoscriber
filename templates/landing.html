{# ==================================================================== FAQ === #}
Questions & answers

Things people ask before signing up.

The short version. For anything deeper, email hello@videoscriber.ai.

Do you train AI models on my content?
No. We never use your uploaded audio, video, transcripts, or recaps to train any model — ours or anyone else's. Our sub-processor agreements with OpenAI and AssemblyAI are zero-retention for the API traffic we send. This is in the Privacy Policy as a contractual commitment, not just a promise.
How long do you keep my uploads?
Raw uploaded video and audio files are automatically deleted 90 days after upload. Transcripts, recaps, and metadata are retained until you delete them or close your account. You can export and delete any content at any time from inside the app.
What happens if I self-host the desktop app?
The open-source desktop app works standalone with no cloud dependency. You bring your own OpenAI and AssemblyAI keys, and all transcripts stay on your machine. You can optionally "Connect to Cloud" to enable backup and our managed processing, but it's off by default.
Does it work in languages other than English?
Transcription supports every language OpenAI Whisper supports (roughly 50 languages with varying quality). Recap drafting currently targets English best. If you need primary support for a specific language, we'd love to hear about it.
How does pricing work?
The cloud app is free to start while we're in beta. We'll publish clear pricing before we leave beta — expect per-minute transcription pricing with a generous free tier and no per-seat fees. The open-source desktop app is and will always be free.
Is there a minimum recording length?
No minimum. Videoscriber handles clips from a few seconds (hallway conversations, voice memos) up to 1 GB per file (roughly 8 hours of audio or 90 minutes of 1080p video).
Can I integrate it with my existing tools?
The cloud app exposes a clean REST API for fetching transcripts and recaps. The desktop app has a plug-in architecture for custom providers and post-processing steps. Webhooks and direct integrations are on the roadmap.
